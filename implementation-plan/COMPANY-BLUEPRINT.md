# Company Blueprint — SignalForge AI

## Company identity

- **Name:** SignalForge AI
- **Type:** AI SaaS startup
- **Core product:** Meeting notes and action extraction SaaS
- **North-star goal (90 days):** Reach 20 paying customers

## Goal hierarchy

### G1 (Company)
Reach 20 paying customers in 90 days.

### G1.1 (Product)
Ship MVP v1 and maintain stable onboarding and core summary flow.

### G1.2 (Growth)
Establish 2 reliable acquisition channels with baseline CAC metrics.

### G1.3 (Customer Success)
Maintain <24h first response and onboarding completion above 60%.

## First 30-day KPIs

- Trial signups/week
- Activation rate (first summary generated)
- Paid conversion rate
- Time-to-first-value
- Weekly burn by agent/team
- Issue throughput (todo → done)
- Approval SLA

## Delivery phases

### Phase 1 (Week 1)
- Set up company, agents, projects, and initial backlog
- Ship MVP skeleton

### Phase 2 (Week 2)
- Wire billing + analytics + first acquisition channel

### Phase 3 (Week 3)
- Hardening + QA + support workflows + second channel

### Phase 4 (Week 4)
- Iterate based on first customer feedback and churn drivers
