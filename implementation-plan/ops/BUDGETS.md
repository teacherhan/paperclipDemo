# Budget Policy (Monthly)

## Initial per-agent budget caps (USD equivalent)

- ceo_01: 80
- cto_01: 120
- eng_fe_01: 150
- eng_be_01: 150
- qa_rel_01: 75
- cmo_01: 100
- growth_01: 150
- support_01: 75

**Total:** 900/month

## Controls

- Warning at 70% utilization
- Escalation warning at 90%
- Hard-stop at 100% (agent pauses until board override)
- Board-only override required for temporary budget extension

## Weekly review

1. Compare spend vs issue throughput by agent.
2. Reduce budgets for low-impact loops.
3. Increase budgets only for bottleneck, high-leverage agents.
4. Record rationale in weekly board memo.
